Welcome to SUSSY FACILITY AND MANAGEMENT SERVICES PVT LTD

Your trusted partner in facility management solutions! We are a newly incorporated (In November 2023) company registered under Startup India and MSME, dedicated to providing top-notch services to ensure your facilities run smoothly, efficiently, and safely. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering exceptional results, tailored to meet your unique needs.

From maintenance and repairs to cleaning and security, pest control and sanitizations, fleet management and transportation support, landscaping, payroll and outsourcing, manpower providing—we offer a comprehensive range of services to support your operational success for sustainable business growth.

At SFMSPL, we prioritize customer satisfaction, reliability, and sustainability. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with our clients, providing them with peace of mind and allowing them to focus on their core business.

Join us in our mission to redefine facility management standards. Let’s work together to create a safe, productive, and thriving environment for your organisation.
